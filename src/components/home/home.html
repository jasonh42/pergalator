What's a PERG?

Pandora encourages all employees to collaborate and socialize with Pandora Employee Resource Groups (PERGs). Employee Groups are voluntary, employee driven groups that are organized around a particular shared interest. We encourage motivated employees to initiate their own PERG. PERGs should be inclusive to all staff participation and promote socialization through a shared interest or hobby.
